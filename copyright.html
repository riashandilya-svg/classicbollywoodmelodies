3. DMCA Takedown Notice : For Rights Holders

If you are a copyright owner, or an authorised representative of one, and you believe that any content on riaaz infringes upon your copyright, you may submit a written notice to us. Upon receipt of a valid notice, we will act promptly — including removal of the relevant content.

To be valid, your notice must include all of the following:

  1. Your full name and contact information (email address, phone number or mailing address).
  2. A description of the copyrighted work you believe has been infringed.
  3. The specific content on riaaz that you believe infringes your work (please include the URL or page name).
  4. A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use of the material is not authorised by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  5. A statement that the information in your notice is accurate, and — under penalty of perjury — that you are the copyright owner or are authorised to act on their behalf.
  6. Your electronic or physical signature.

4. Counter-Notice

If you believe content was removed from riaaz as a result of a mistake or misidentification, you may submit a counter-notice to the same email address above. Your counter-notice should include:

  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good-faith belief the content was removed as a result of mistake or misidentification.

We will review all counter-notices in good faith and respond within a reasonable time.
