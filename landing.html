रियाज़

In Indian classical tradition, riyaaz is the sacred daily practice, the hours a musician puts in before the world wakes up. It is not performance. It is devotion. It is the quiet love of returning to the same note, again and again, until sound becomes soul.

We named this riaaz with one intentional shift, because Ria lives within it. She built this on a simple, steadfast belief that practice, done with love and patience, becomes music long before anyone hears it.

This is where discipline becomes poetry. Where returning each day is an act of love.
