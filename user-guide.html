Connect Your Digital Piano / Keyboard to Your Laptop (USB-MIDI Setup)

▼

You can use this app on a laptop, tablet, or mobile device. You can also play notes using your mouse or touch. For the best experience, connect a digital piano or MIDI keyboard so the app can detect the notes you play and display them instantly on screen.

Good to know: In most cases, this is “plug-and-play.” Many keyboards support USB-MIDI and your device will recognize them automatically.

What you need

1) A digital piano / MIDI keyboard
2) A USB cable (usually USB-B on the keyboard)
3) Your laptop (USB-A or USB-C port)

Most keyboards have a square-ish USB-B port (often labeled “USB to Host”, “USB”, or “MIDI USB”). Your laptop side may be USB-A (classic rectangle) or USB-C (small oval).

Make sure you buy the right cable:
• If your laptop has USB-A: get USB-A → USB-B
• If your laptop has USB-C only: get USB-C → USB-B (or use a USB-C hub + USB-A → USB-B cable)
• If your keyboard has only round 5-pin MIDI ports (no USB): you’ll need a USB-MIDI interface (MIDI IN/OUT).

Step-by-step setup

1

Find the correct port on your keyboard

Turn the keyboard around and look for a port labeled USB, USB to Host, or MIDI USB. This is usually a USB-B port (square-ish, “printer style”).

2

Connect the cable (keyboard first, then laptop)

Plug the USB-B end into your keyboard, then plug the other end into your laptop (USB-A or USB-C, depending on your cable/adapter).

3

Power on the keyboard

Turn the keyboard on. Some models only “announce” themselves to the computer after they’re powered on, so if the app doesn’t detect it immediately, power-cycle the keyboard once.

4

Open the app and allow MIDI access (if prompted)

In some browsers/devices you may see a prompt asking to allow a MIDI device. Click Allow. Once connected, the app will detect your playing and show the notes on screen.

5

Play a few notes to confirm

Play single notes and then a chord. You should see the corresponding notes appear in the app immediately. If nothing shows up, use the troubleshooting tips below.

Drivers and common installation notes

Most keyboards: No driver needed. They are “class-compliant” USB-MIDI devices and work automatically.
  • Windows: Usually installs the device automatically the first time you plug it in. If Windows shows an error, or your keyboard brand provides a dedicated “USB-MIDI driver,” install the driver from the manufacturer’s official support page, then unplug/replug the keyboard and try again.
  • Mac: Typically no driver needed for USB-MIDI. Just plug in the keyboard and refresh/reopen the browser tab if needed.
  • Chromebook: Many USB-MIDI keyboards work, but compatibility can vary by model and browser settings. If it’s not detected, try a different browser or a powered USB-C hub.
  • Tablets / phones: You may need an OTG adapter (USB-C OTG or Lightning to USB adapter). Once connected, allow MIDI/USB access when prompted.

Troubleshooting (quick fixes)

1

Try a different USB port or cable

Bad cables are more common than you’d think. If you’re using a hub, try plugging directly into the laptop (or try another hub).

2

Unplug → close the browser tab → reconnect

Some browsers only detect MIDI devices when the page loads. Close the app tab, reconnect the keyboard, then reopen the page.

3

Check the keyboard’s USB mode (if available)

A few keyboards have a setting related to USB/MIDI. If your keyboard has a “USB Mode,” set it to MIDI or USB-MIDI.

4

Install the manufacturer driver (Windows, only if needed)

If your keyboard is not recognized or shows warnings in Windows, download and install the official driver for your exact model, restart, then reconnect.

Still stuck? Contact support and include: your keyboard model name, your device (Windows/Mac/Chromebook), and whether you’re using a hub/adapter.

How to Verify Book Codes

▼

Follow these simple steps to verify your physical book and unlock book owner benefits:

1

Scan the QR Code

Open your phone's camera and scan the QR code on your physical book. This will take you to the book code generation page.

2

Get Your Unique Code

You'll be redirected to get-book-code.html where you'll receive a unique verification ID.

3

Click "Generate My Code"

Click the button to generate your personal verification code. Save this code!

4

Go to Verification Page

Navigate to verify-book.html and paste your unique code.

5

Upload Photo Proof

Upload a clear photo of your book as proof of ownership. This helps us prevent fraud.

6

Optional: Upload Video (Earn 1 Credit!)

You can optionally upload a video review to earn 1 free song credit. Please read the video consent terms before uploading.

That's it! Once verified, you'll unlock book owner pricing. The verify page will no longer be accessible as you're already verified. You'll continue to receive book owner discounts on all future purchases.
Video Bonus: Videos earn you 1 credit that can be redeemed for any song. By uploading a video, you agree to the terms outlined in our video consent policy.
One-Time Verification: You only need to verify once. After verification, you permanently unlock book owner pricing and the verify page will no longer appear in your account.

How Bundles Work

▼

Bundles are a cost-effective way to unlock multiple songs at once. Here's everything you need to know:

What is a bundle?
A bundle gives you 5 song credits at a discounted price. Each credit unlocks 1 song of your choice.

Key Features:

  • Choose ANY song: Credits work across all books and all songs. No restrictions!
  • No expiration: Your credits never expire. Use them whenever you want.
  • Available to everyone: Both book owners and non-book owners can purchase bundles.
  • Best value: Bundles offer the lowest per-song price compared to individual purchases.
  • Book owner discount: Book owners get bundles at a discounted rate compared to non-book owners.

How to Use Your Credits:

1

Purchase a Bundle

Buy a 5-song bundle from the main site or your dashboard. Book owners get a discounted price.

2

Credits Added Automatically

Your 5 credits are instantly added to your account. Check your dashboard to see your balance.

3

Choose Any Song

Browse any book and select any song you like. No restrictions!

4

Redeem with Credit

At checkout, choose "Use Credit" instead of paying. One credit will be deducted automatically.

Pricing Example (INR):
Book Owner Bundle: ₹550 for 5 songs (₹110 per song)
Non-Book Owner Bundle: Higher price, but still better value than individual purchases

Frequently Asked Questions

▼
Q: How long does book verification take?
A: Book verification is usually instant! Once you submit your code and photo, the system automatically verifies your book and updates your account immediately.
Q: Can I verify multiple books?
A: You only need to verify once. After your first verification, you permanently unlock book owner pricing and the verify page will no longer be accessible. You don't need to verify additional books.
Q: What if my QR code doesn't work?
A: If the QR code isn't scanning, you can manually visit the book code generation page and follow the steps. If you continue to have issues, contact support.
Q: Do bundle credits expire?
A: No! Your credits never expire. Use them whenever you're ready to unlock new songs.
Q: Can I use credits for any song in any book?
A: Absolutely! Credits work across all four books with no restrictions. Mix and match songs from different books as you like.
Q: Can non-book owners purchase bundles?
A: Yes! Both book owners and non-book owners can purchase bundles. The difference is that book owners get bundles at a discounted price.
Q: What happens to my credits if I buy more songs individually?
A: Your credits stay in your account. You can choose whether to use a credit or pay for each song individually.
Q: How do I earn the video bonus credit?
A: During book verification, you'll have the option to upload a video review. If you do, you'll receive 1 free credit (worth 1 song). Make sure to read the video consent terms first.
Q: Can I get a refund on bundle credits?
A: Bundle purchases are final and non-refundable. However, since credits never expire, you can use them at your own pace.
Q: What's the difference between book owner and non-book owner pricing?
A: Book owners get discounted pricing on both individual songs AND bundles. Non-book owners pay standard pricing but can still purchase bundles at a higher price. See the pricing page for details.
Q: Can I share my account with friends or family?
A: Each account is personal and tied to one email address. Account sharing is not permitted as it violates our terms of service.
Q: How do I track my progress on songs?
A: Visit your dashboard to see all your purchased songs. You can mark each song as "Not Started," "In Progress," or "Completed" to track your learning journey.
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We use Razorpay as our payment processor, which accepts all major credit/debit cards, UPI (for Indian users), net banking, and various wallets.
Q: Will I lose book owner pricing if I don't verify all my books?
A: No! Once you verify any book, you permanently unlock book owner pricing. You don't need to verify multiple books to maintain your discount.
