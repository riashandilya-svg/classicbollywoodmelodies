riaaz is created and operated by Ria Om Shandilya.

By using riaaz, you consent to the practices described below.

To provide a seamless experience, we collect limited and necessary information, including:

Your information is used thoughtfully and only where necessary, including to:

4. Sharing of Information

Your trust is important to us. We do not sell your personal data.

Information is shared only with carefully selected service providers:

All partners operate under strict privacy and data protection standards.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure authentication, and PCI-compliant payment processing.

While every reasonable measure is taken, no digital system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Cookies

riaaz uses essential cookies and local storage to maintain your session and preferences.

We do not use advertising or intrusive tracking technologies. Disabling cookies may affect certain functionality.

You retain full control over your information. You may:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request corrections where necessary
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at riaomshandilya@gmail.com.

riaaz is designed to be accessible for learners of all ages, including younger students.

Where a user is under the age of 13, we expect that access to riaaz is provided under the supervision of a parent or guardian.

We do not knowingly collect more personal information than is necessary to provide the service. Parents or guardians may contact us at any time to review or request deletion of a child’s information.
